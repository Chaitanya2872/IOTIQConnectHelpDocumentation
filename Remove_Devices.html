Remove Device

Follow these steps to Remove Device
  1. Click Remove or Move Device
  2. Select Desired Devices you want to remove
  3. Click on Remove Devices
  4. Confirm Removal by clicking on Yes, Remove
  5. Selected Devices will be deleted from the Space

What Happens When You Remove a Device

  • Device Status: Device is removed from your space only, not from your account
  • Connections: All assigned switches and connections will be unassigned
  • Reassignment: You will need to reassign connections when re-adding the device
Tip
  • If you plan to move a device to another space, first remove it from the current space, then add it to the new space as a new device
