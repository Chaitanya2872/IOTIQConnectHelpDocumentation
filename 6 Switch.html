6 Switch - User Guide

The 6 Switch allows control of six assigned switches, which can be linked to any listed appliances.

Getting Started

  1. Turn on your 6 Switch device
  2. Open the IOTIQ mobile application on your smartphone
  3. Follow the Device Setup Flow to pair this switch to your mobile app
  4. Assign your switches to the desired appliances
  5. Confirm your setup and begin using your switch
Device Setup Screen
Tip:
  • Place your switch in an easily accessible location
  • Keep your switch within range of your Wi-Fi router
  • Ensure your smartphone is connected to the same Wi-Fi network during setup

Basic Operations

Toggle appliances on/off using the mobile application.

Mobile App Interface

Key Functions

  • Short press the top button to toggle the first assigned appliance
  • Short press the bottom button to toggle the second assigned appliance

Advanced Features

Rename switches for easy identification in your app

Control your devices from anywhere through the IOTIQ mobile application

Note:

Troubleshooting

  • Unresponsive Switch: If the switch isn't responding, check if it's in setup mode (blinking blue light). If in setup mode, reset the device and try again.
  • Connection Issues: Ensure your Wi-Fi network is functioning properly. Move the switch closer to your router if needed.
  • Pairing Problems: If pairing fails, restart both your switch and mobile app, then try again.
  • App Crashes: Update to the latest app version and restart your phone.
Important:
  • Do not expose the switch to water or extreme temperatures
  • Factory reset will erase all settings and paired devices
  • Only use the provided power adapter to avoid damage
