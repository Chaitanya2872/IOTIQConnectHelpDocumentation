Curtain Controller

A Curtain Controller automates the opening and closing of curtains via remote control, smartphone app, or voice commands.

Getting Started

  1. Install the Controller as per manufacturer instructions.
  2. Connect to Power and ensure proper wiring.
  3. Connect input and output terminals properly.
  4. Open the IOTIQ mobile application on your smartphone
  5. Pair with App/Remote for control setup.
  6. Test Operation using open/close commands.
Tip:
  • Set Schedules for energy savings and convenience.
  • Keep Tracks Clean to prevent motor strain.
  • Use Smart Integration for hands-free control.

Basic Operations

  • use the toggle on and off to control the curtain

Key Functions

  • Works with compatible smart home systems.
  • Requires a stable power source for smooth operation.

Advanced Features

Voice Control

Integrates with voice assistants like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, or Siri, allowing hands-free operation.

Curtain Modes

Set to Open, Mid Open , Close based on your convenience

Scheduling

Opens/closes at set times or syncs with sunrise/sunset.

Light & Motion Sensors

Adjusts based on sunlight or movement for efficiency and security.

Note:
  • Needs Wi-Fi/Bluetooth for app control.
  • Ensure proper installation to avoid motor strain.
  • Regular maintenance improves lifespan.

Troubleshooting

  • Curtain doesn’t respond : Check power, connectivity, and app settings.
  • Curtains stop midway : Inspect for obstructions or motor issues.
  • Remote not working : Replace batteries and re-pair.
  • App Crashes : Update to the latest app version and restart your phone.
Important:
  • Avoid manual pulling to prevent damage.
  • Ensure power safety during installation.
  • Keep away from children to prevent accidents.
