Add a Device to Your Room

Follow these steps to add a device to your room.
  • Navigate to your Home Screen.
  • Click on Add New Device on the Home Screen.
  • Select the Item Type from the available categories.
  • Choose the specific Item you want to add.
  • Select the desired Device from the list.
  • Click on Setup Device until the device becomes visible on the network or app interface.
  • Enter Wi-Fi SSID and Password to which Device is connected
  • Once visible, click on Continue to Home to complete the process.
Warning:
  • Ensure the device is powered on and within range of the network before proceeding.
Note: The device must be already onboarded and registered in the system.
Tip: Verify the device status (online or available for connection) before attempting to add it to your room.
