Assign Switches

Follow these steps to assign switches to your devices.
  1. After Adding a Device in room, click on Assign Switches
  2. Click on Assign New Device
  3. Enter a Name for your Switch
  4. Select an appliance from Dropdown, such as fan, light, Dimmer, AC, Curtain, etc.
  5. Click on Assign and Continue
  6. After assigning, all Switches will be added in Home Screen
Note:
  • User has the flexibility to assign only a few switches by selecting None from the appliance dropdown
  • You can Rename, Unnasign and Move a Switch to other room by clicking on three dots in internal remote

Benefits of Assigned Switches

  • You can assign switch to any of the other existing devices in the room
  • Create Scenes for different settings
  • View usage reports for better energy management
Tip:
  • Create meaningful names for your switches to easily identify them
  • Group similar switches together for easier management
  • Use Scenes for common activities like "Movie Night" or "Reading" for quick adjustments
