ACE Heavy Duty

An ACE Heavy Duty Switch is likely a high-power electrical switch which are built to handle high voltage and current loads, ensuring durability and reliability.

Getting Started

  1. Check the voltage and current rating before installation.
  2. Connect wires to the input and output terminals as per the manual.
  3. Open the IOTIQ mobile application on your smartphone
  4. Follow the Device Setup Flow to pair this switch to your mobile app
  5. Turn on the power to test functionality
Device Setup Screen
Tip:
  • Mount the switch in the desired location.
  • Keep your switch within range of your Wi-Fi router
  • Ensure your smartphone is connected to the same Wi-Fi network during setup

Basic Operations

  • Use the power button on the dimmer or control it via the app.
  • Some models have an LED indicator to show power status.
  • Use the app slider to set brightness levels.
Mobile App Interface

Key Functions

  • Short press the top button to toggle the first assigned appliance
  • Short press the bottom button to toggle the second assigned appliance

Troubleshooting

  • Switch Not Working : Check if the power supply is active and wiring is correctly connected
  • Overheating Issue : Reduce the connected load if the switch feels excessively hot
  • Flickering : Verify load capacity and ensure it does not exceed the switch's rating.
  • App Crashes : Update to the latest app version and restart your phone.
Important:
  • Inspect for blown fuses or tripped circuit breakers.
  • Built-in safety mechanisms to prevent overheating and electrical surges
  • Factory reset will erase all settings and paired devices
