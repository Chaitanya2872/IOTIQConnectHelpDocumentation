How to Move a Device Between Rooms

Follow these steps to move a device from one room to another within the same space.
  • Click on Remove or Move Device
  • Select the device(s) you want to move
  • Select the destination room in the Floor
  • Click on Continue to complete the move
Warning: You cannot directly move a device from one space to another space.

Moving Between Spaces

To move a device between different spaces:

  1. First remove the device from the current space
  2. Switch to the desired space
  3. Add the device as a new device in the new space
Tip: Make sure you have the necessary permissions in both the source and destination rooms before attempting to move devices.
