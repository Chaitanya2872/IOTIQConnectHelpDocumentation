How to Add a Device into Your Account

Follow these steps to add a new device to your account.
  • Click on the Menu Bar.
  • Click on Scan QR Code.
  • Click Grant Permission.
  • Scan the QR Code of a specific device.
Warning: Ensure the device is not already in another account.
  • If it is a new device, it will be added to your account automatically.
  • If the device is already in another user's account, you need to transfer the device from the account of the current owner.
Note: Contact the original owner if you need to transfer the device.
Tip: Keep your camera permissions enabled for a seamless experience.
