Setup a Space

Follow these steps to setup a space .
  1. Click Setup Space
  2. Select Space Type (e.g., Home, Office, Hostel, Hotel, etc.)
  3. Add a Space Name
  4. Add your Address
  5. Click on Continue

Alternative Methods to Add Space

  • You can also add spaces through My Spaces Dropdown
  • You can also add a space by clicking on Add new Space in Manage Spaces
Note
  • Creation of Space allows you to Add Rooms and control devices
  • Each space can have its own unique name and type
Tip
  • Use descriptive space names for easy identification
  • Select the appropriate Space type to access relevant device templates
  • You can change your spaces from My Spaces Dropdown in Home Screen
