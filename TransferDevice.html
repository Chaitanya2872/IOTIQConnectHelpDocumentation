How to Transfer a Device to Your Account

Follow these steps to transfer an existing device to your account.
  • Click on the Menu Bar.
  • Click on Scan QR Code.
  • Click Grant Permission.
  • Scan the QR Code of the device you want to transfer.
  • Enter the OTP received by the current device owner.
Warning: Make sure you have permission from the current owner before initiating the transfer.
  • Once the correct OTP is entered, the device will be transferred to your account automatically.
Note: The current owner must be available to provide the OTP for a successful transfer.
Tip: Ensure camera permissions are enabled and scan the correct QR for the desired device.
