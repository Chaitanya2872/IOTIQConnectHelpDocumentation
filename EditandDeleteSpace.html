How to Edit and Delete a Space

Follow these steps to edit or delete space.

How to Edit a Room

  1. Click on Menu Bar
  2. Click on Spaces
  3. Click on the Edit of the Space you want to edit
  4. Rename the Space by tapping on Add Space Name
  5. Select Space Typefrom dropdwon
  6. Click on Save

How to Delete a Room

  1. Click on Menu Bar
  2. Click on Spaces
  3. Click on Delete of the space you want to delete
  4. Click on Yes, Remove to Confirm the Deletion
Important:
  • Deletion of Space leads to Delete all the rooms and devices you added in that space but not from your account
  • You cannot delete the space you are currently in
  • You will need to add new rooms and reassign devices if you create a new space later.
Tip:
  • Consider moving devices to another space before deleting a space
  • Take note of your device configurations before deleting a space
  • Editing a space name or type will not affect the devices assigned to it
