Limitations for Secondary and Guest Users

Secondary User

When joined as a Secondary User:

Spaces & Rooms:
  • Can add, edit, and delete spaces and rooms.
Scenes:
  • Can create, edit, and delete scenes.
Devices:
  • Can control all devices.
  • Can add new devices that are in the Primary User's account.
  • Can rename and delete devices.
Restrictions:
  • Cannot add devices owned by their own account to the Primary User's account.
  • Cannot transfer devices from the Primary User's account without their permission.
  • Can transfer devices they own to the Primary User by scanning a QR code.
Guest User
Spaces & Rooms:
  • Can switch between spaces and rooms assigned by the Primary User.
Devices:
  • Can control assigned devices.
Scenes:
  • Can control assigned scenes.
Restrictions:
  • Cannot add, edit, or delete spaces, rooms, devices, or scenes.
Note:
  • Primary Users maintain full control over all aspects of the project.
  • Guest User permissions can be modified by the Primary User at any time.
