How to Remove a Device

Follow these steps to remove a device from a room in your space.
  • Click on Remove or Move Device
  • Select the device(s) you want to remove in that room
  • Click on Remove Devices
  • Click on Yes, Remove to confirm the removal

What Happens When You Remove a Device

  • Device Status: Device is removed from your space only, not from your account
  • Connections: All assigned switches and connections will be unassigned
  • Reassignment: You will need to reassign connections when re-adding the device
Tip: If you plan to move a device to another space, first remove it from the current space, then add it to the new space as a new device.
